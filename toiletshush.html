About Toilet Shush

Toilet Shush is a simple, practical privacy app that generates ambient sounds to mask bathroom noises in public or shared restrooms. Whether you're at the office, a friend's house, or traveling, Toilet Shush gives you peace of mind with a single tap. Just open the app, tap to start, and a soothing ambient sound plays to cover any unwanted noise.

Inspired by Japan's "Sound Princess" (otohime) devices found in many public restrooms, Toilet Shush brings the same concept to your pocket. No more running the faucet or flushing multiple times, just tap and relax. The app includes multiple sound options and a clean, discreet interface designed for immediate use when you need it most.

Frequently Asked Questions

How does Toilet Shush work?
Open the app, tap the play button, and ambient sound plays through your phone's speaker to mask bathroom noises. It's that simple, one tap for instant privacy.
Does Toilet Shush need internet?
No. All sounds are stored locally on your device and work completely offline. You can use Toilet Shush anywhere, even without WiFi or mobile data.
How does it provide privacy?
The app allows you to choose from different ambient sounds to mask noises, giving you peace of mind in any shared or public restroom.
What sounds are available?
Toilet Shush includes options like white noise, running water, and other ambient sounds designed to effectively mask bathroom noises while remaining pleasant and discreet.
What is a "Sound Princess"?
A Sound Princess (otohime) is a device commonly found in Japanese restrooms that plays flushing or running water sounds for privacy. Toilet Shush brings this same concept to your phone as a portable, always-available alternative.
